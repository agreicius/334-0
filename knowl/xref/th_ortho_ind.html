Theorem 1.19.2. Orthogonal implies independent.

Let \(S=(v_i)_{i\in I}\) be a tuple in the inner product space \((V,\angvec{\, ,})\text{.}\) If \(S\) is orthogonal, then \(S\) is linearly independent. Using logical shorthand:
\begin{equation} S \text{ orthogonal}\implies S \text{ linearly independent}\text{.}\tag{1.67} \end{equation}
