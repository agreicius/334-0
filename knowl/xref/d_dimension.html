Definition 1.8.1. Dimension.

Let \(V\) be a vector space. The dimension of \(V\), denoted \(\dim V\text{,}\) is defined as follows:
  • If \(V\) is infinite dimensional, then \(\dim V=\infty\text{,}\) as before.
  • If \(V\) is finite dimensional, then we define \(\dim V\) to be the length of any basis of \(V\text{:}\) i.e., if \(B=(v_i)_{i\in I}\) is a basis of \(V\text{,}\) then \(\dim V=\len B=\abs{I}\text{.}\)
