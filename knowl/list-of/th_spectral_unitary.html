Theorem 1.23.15. Spectral theorem for unitary operators: \(F=\C\).

Let \(F=\C\text{,}\) let \((V,\angvec{\, ,})\) be a finite-dimensional complex inner product space, and let \(T\in \mathcal{L}(V)\text{.}\) The following statements are equivalent.
  1. \(T\) is unitary
  2. \(T\) is normal and \(\abs{\lambda}=1\) for all eigenvalues \(\lambda\) of \(T\text{.}\)
