Example 1.12.13. Coordinate vector map.

Let \(B=(v_i)_{i\in I}\) be a basis of the vector space \(V\) and define
\begin{align*} T\colon V\amp \rightarrow F^I\\ v \amp \longmapsto [v]_B\text{.} \end{align*}
  1. Show that \(T\) is an injective linear transformation.
  2. Prove that if \(\dim V< \infty\text{,}\) then \(T\) is an isomorphism.
  3. Given an example where \(T\) is not an isomorphism.
