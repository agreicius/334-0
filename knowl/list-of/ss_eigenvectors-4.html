Convention 1.15.3. Eigenvectors of matrix transformations.

Let \(A\in F^{n,n}\) be a square matrix. As a form of conflation of \(A\) with its corresponding matrix transformation \(T_A\colon F^n\rightarrow F^n\text{,}\) we will call \(\boldx\) an eigenvector of \(A\) if it is an eigenvalue of \(T_A\text{;}\) similarly, we will call \(\lambda\) an eigenvalue of \(A\) if it is an eigenvalue of \(T_A\text{.}\)
