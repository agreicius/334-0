Definition 1.12.12. Coordinate vectors.

Let \(B=(v_i)_{i\in I}\) be a basis of the vector space \(V\text{.}\) Given \(v\in V\text{,}\) let \(v=\sum_{i\in I}c_iv_i\) be the unique expression of \(v\) as a linear combination of the \(v_i\text{.}\) We define the tuple of scalars \((c_i)_{i\in I}\in F^I\) to be the coordinate vector of \(v\) with respect to \(B\text{,}\) denoted \([v]_B\text{:}\) i.e.,
\begin{equation} [v]_B=(c_i)_{i\in I}\text{.}\tag{1.30} \end{equation}
