Definition 1.18.6. Polynomial evaluation.

Let \(F\in \{\R, \C\}\text{,}\) and let \(n\) be a positive integer. Given any distinct scalars \(c_1,c_2,\dots, c_{n+1}\in F\text{,}\) the function
\begin{align*} \angvec{\phantom{v},\phantom{v}}\colon P_n(F)\times P_n(F) \amp \rightarrow F\\ (f,g) \amp \mapsto \angvec{f,g}=\sum_{i=1}^{n+1}f(c_i)\overline{g(c_i)} \end{align*}
is an inner product on \(P_n(F)\) . We call \(\angvec{\, ,}\) a polynomial evaluation inner product.
