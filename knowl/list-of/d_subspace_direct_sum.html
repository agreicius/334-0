Definition 1.5.4. Direct sum of subspaces.

Let \(W_1,W_2,\dots, W_n\) be subspaces of the \(F\)-vector space \(V\text{,}\) and let \(W=\sum_{i=1}^n\text{.}\) We say \(W\) is the direct sum of the the subspaces \(W_i\text{,}\) denoted \(W=W_1\oplus W_2+\cdots \oplus W_n\) or \(W=\bigoplus_{i=1}^n W_i\text{,}\) if the following property holds: given vectors \(v_i, v_i'\in W_i\text{,}\) \(1\leq i\leq n\text{,}\) we have
\begin{equation*} v_1+v_2+\cdots +v_n=v_1'+v_2'+\cdots +v_n' \end{equation*}
if and only if
\begin{equation*} v_i=v_i' \end{equation*}
for all \(1\leq i\leq n\text{.}\)
