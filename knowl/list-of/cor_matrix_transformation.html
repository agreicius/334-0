Corollary 1.9.21. Matrix transformations.

Let \(T\colon F^n\rightarrow F^m\) be a linear transformation. There is a unique matrix \(A\in F^{m,n}\) such that \(T=T_A\text{.}\) In particular, all linear transformations from \(F^n\) to \(F^m\) are matrix transformations.

Proof.

The proof is left as an exercise.
