Example 1.18.11. Norm.

  1. Weighted dot product.
    Consider the weighted dot product on \(F^n\) with weights \(k_1,k_2,\dots, k_n\text{.}\) Given \(\boldx=(x_1,x_2,\dots, x_n)\in F^n\text{,}\) we have
    \begin{align*} \norm{\boldx} \amp = \sqrt{\angvec{\boldx,\boldx}} \amp (\text{def. of norm}) \\ \amp = \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^nk_ix_i\overline{x_i}}\\ \amp = \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^n k_i\abs{x_i}^2} \amp (z\overline{z}=\abs{z}^2)\text{.} \end{align*}
  2. Polynomial evaluation.
    Consider a polynomial evaluation inner product on \(P_n(F)\) with distinct inputs \(c_1,c_2,\dots, c_{n+1}\text{.}\) Given \(f\in P_n(F)\text{,}\) we have
    \begin{align*} \norm{f} \amp =\sqrt{\angvec{f,f}} \amp (\text{def. of norm}) \\ \amp =\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n=1}f(c_i)\overline{f(c_i)}}\\ \amp =\sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n=1}\abs{f(c_i)}^2} \amp (z\overline{z}=\abs{z}^2) \end{align*}
  3. Integral inner product.
    Consider the integral inner product on \(C(I)\text{,}\) where \(I=[a,b]\) is a closed interval in \(\R\text{.}\) Given \(f\in C^(I)\text{,}\) we have
    \begin{align*} \norm{f} \amp =\sqrt{\angvec{f,f}} \amp (\text{def. of norm})\\ \amp = \sqrt{\int_a^b f(x)f(x)\, dx}\\ \amp = \sqrt{\int_a^b f^2(x)\, dx}\text{.} \end{align*}
