Theorem 1.1.25. Properties of fields.

Let \(F\) be a field, let \(\alpha,\beta\in F\text{,}\) and let \(m,n\in \Z_{\geq 0}\text{.}\)
  1. \(0\alpha=0\text{.}\)
  2. If \(\alpha\beta=0\text{,}\) then \(\alpha=0\) or \(\beta=0\text{.}\)
  3. \((\alpha\beta)^{m}=\alpha^{m}\beta^{m}\text{.}\)
  4. \(\alpha^{m+n}=\alpha^{m}\alpha^{n}\text{.}\)
  5. \((\alpha^{m})^{n}=\alpha^{mn}\text{.}\)
  6. \(\alpha^{-m}=(\alpha^{m})^{-1}\) (assuming \(\alpha\ne 0\)).
  7. \((\alpha^{-1})^{-1}=\alpha\) (assuming \(\alpha\ne 0\)).

Proof.
