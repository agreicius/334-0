Theorem 1.6.13. Span and independence.

Let \(S=(v_i)_{i\in I}\) be a nonempty tuple of vectors of the vector space \(V\text{.}\) The following statements are equivalent.
  1. \(S\) is linearly dependent.
  2. There is an index \(i_{0}\in I\) such that \(v_{i_{0}}\) can be written as a linear combination of the other \(v_i\)â€™s: i.e., there exists \(i_0\in I\) such that \(v_{i_0}\in \Span(v_i)_{i\in I-\{i_0\}}\text{.}\) Furthermore, in this case we have
    \begin{equation} \Span(v_i)_{i\in I}=\Span(v_i)_{i\in I-\{i_0\}}\text{.}\tag{1.16} \end{equation}
