Definition 1.21.6. Conjugate and adjoint of a matrix.

Let \(F\in \{\R,\C\}\text{,}\) and let \(A=[a_{ij}]\in F^{m,n}\text{.}\)
The conjugate of \(A\text{,}\) denoted \(\overline{A}\) is the \(m\times n\) matrix defined as
\begin{equation*} \overline{A}=[\overline{a_{ij}}]\in F^{m,n}\text{.} \end{equation*}
The adjoint of \(A\text{,}\) denoted \(A^*\text{,}\) is the \(n\times m\) matrix defined as
\begin{equation*} A^*=(\overline{A})^T=\overline{(A^T)}\text{.} \end{equation*}
