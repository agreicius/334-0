Theorem 1.23.14. Unitary operators.

Let \(F\in \{\R, \C\}\text{,}\) let \((V, \angvec{\, ,})\) be a finite-dimensional inner product space, and let \(T\in \mathcal{L}(V)\text{.}\) The following statements are equivalent.
  1. \(T\) is unitary.
  2. \(\norm{T(v)}=\norm{v}\) for all \(v\in V\text{.}\)
  3. \(\angvec{T(v),T(w)}=\angvec{v,w}\) for all \(v,w\in V\text{.}\)
