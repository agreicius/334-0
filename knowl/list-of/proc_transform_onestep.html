Procedure 1.9.11. One-step technique for transformations.

Let \(T\colon V\rightarrow W\) be a function between vector spaces. To prove \(T\) is a linear transformation, show that
\begin{equation*} T(cv_1+dv_2)=cT(v_1)+dT(v_2) \end{equation*}
for all scalars \(c, d\in R\) and all vectors \(v_1,v_2\in V\text{.}\)
