Definition 1.7.9. Length of a tuple.

Let \(S=(a_i)_{i\in I}\) be a tuple of elements of the set \(A\text{.}\)
  • We say the tuple \(S\) is finite (resp. infinite) if its indexing set \(I\) is finite (resp. infinite).
  • If \(I\) is finite of cardinality \(\abs{I}=n\text{,}\) then we say \(S\) is a tuple of length \(n\text{.}\)
