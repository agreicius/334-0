Definition 1.7.12. Length of a tuple.

Let \(S=(a_i)_{i\in I}\) be a tuple of elements of the set \(A\text{.}\)
  • If \(I\) is finite, with cardinality \(\abs{I}=n\text{,}\) then we say \(S\) is finite and of length \(n\text{,}\) denoted \(\len S=n\text{.}\)
  • If \(I\) is infinite, then we say \(S\) is infinite, or of infinite length, denoted \(\len S=\infty\text{.}\)
