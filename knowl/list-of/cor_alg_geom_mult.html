Corollary 1.24.5.

Let \(T\in \mathcal{L}(V)\text{,}\) where \(V\) is finite dimensional, let \(f\in P(F)\) be the characteristic polynomial of \(T\text{.}\) Let \(\lambda\) be an eigenvalue of \(T\) of algebraic multiplicity \(m\text{:}\) i.e., suppose \(f\) factors as
\begin{equation*} f(x)=(x-\lambda)^mg(x)\text{,} \end{equation*}
where \(g(\lambda)\ne 0\text{.}\) The geometric multiplicity of \(\lambda\) is bounded above by the algebraic multiplicity: i.e.,
\begin{equation} \dim E(\lambda, T)\leq m\text{.}\tag{1.121} \end{equation}
