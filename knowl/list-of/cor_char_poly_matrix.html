Corollary 1.16.6. Characteristic polynomial of a matrix.

Let \(A\in F^{n,n}\text{,}\) and let \(f(x)=x^n+a_{n-1}x+\cdots +a_1x+a_0\) be the characteristic polynomial of \(A\text{.}\)
  1. \(\lambda\in F\) is an eigenvalue of of \(T_A\) if and only if \(f\) factors over \(F\) as
    \begin{equation*} f(x)=(x-\lambda)g(x)\text{,} \end{equation*}
    for some \(g\in P_{n-1}(F)\text{.}\)
  2. \(T_A\) has at most \(n\) distinct eigenvalues.
