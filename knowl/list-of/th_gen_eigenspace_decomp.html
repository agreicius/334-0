Theorem 1.25.3. Generalized eigenspace decomposition.

Let \(T\in \mathcal{L}(V)\text{,}\) where \(V\) is finite dimensional, and assume the characteristic polynomial \(f\) of \(T\) factors as
\begin{equation*} f(x)=\prod_{i=1}^r(x-\lambda_i)^{n_i}\text{,} \end{equation*}
where \(\lambda_1,\lambda_2,\dots, \lambda_r\) are the distinct eigenvalues of \(T\text{.}\) We have the following direct sum decomposition of \(V\text{:}\)
\begin{equation} V=\oplus_{i=1}^rG(\lambda_i, T)\text{.}\tag{1.126} \end{equation}

Proof.
